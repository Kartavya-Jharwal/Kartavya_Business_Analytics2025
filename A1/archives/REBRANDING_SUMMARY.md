### 3. Demo Investment Framework

**Example Analysis Included:**

**Question:** Where to invest $100M in green infrastructure?

**Methodology:**
1. Filter by GDP category (Medium/High for capacity)
2. Screen for legally binding commitments (In law/Achieved)
3. Analyze emissions trends (declining = policy effectiveness)
4. Compare R² values (correlation strength)

**Sample Results:**
- ✅ **France:** High GDP, legal commitment, nuclear decoupling → **Low Risk**
- ✅ **Germany:** High GDP, legal commitment, energy transition → **Low Risk**
- ⚠️ **UAE:** High GDP, proposal stage only → **Medium Risk**
- ❌ **Saudi Arabia:** High GDP, no commitment → **High Risk**
